## About the "Other" Category

The "other" category (13 words) contains words that don't fit neatly into standard grammatical categories. These are important grammatical elements:

### Subcategories within "Other":

#### **Conjunctions** (2 words)
Connects sentences or clauses:
- **でも** - "but, however"
- **そして** - "and then"

#### **Copula** (1 word)
The "to be" verb with unique grammatical role:
- **です** - "is, am, are"

#### **Interjections** (4 words)
Exclamations and response words:
- **ええと** - "um" (filler word)
- **あ** - "ah, oh" (exclamation)
- **いいえ** - "no" (response)
- **はい** - "yes" (response)

#### **Determiners** (6 words)
Demonstratives that modify nouns directly:
- **この** - "this"
- **その** - "that"
- **あの** - "that (over there)"
- **どの** - "which"
- **どんな** - "what kind of"
- **つぎの** - "next"

### Note on Categorization
These words are grouped as "other" for simplicity in a vocabulary study context, as they are grammatical elements that learners encounter but don't need to drill in the same way as content words (nouns, verbs, adjectives). However, they are essential for building sentences and natural conversation.
