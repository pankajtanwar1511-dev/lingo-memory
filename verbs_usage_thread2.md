# 🔤 N5 Verbs Usage Patterns - Thread 2

**Verbs 55-108** | 着る (きる) → 止める (とめる)

---

## 📖 How to Use This Guide

✅ Each verb shows **kana-only** usage patterns
✅ Focus on **particle patterns** (を, に, で, と, が)
✅ These are **authentic collocations** used by native speakers
✅ Perfect for **beginner to intermediate** learners

## 5️⃣5️⃣ 着る (きる) — *to wear*

**Usage Patterns:**

1. ふくを きる — wear clothes
2. ティーシャツを きる — wear a T-shirt
3. せいふくを きる — wear a uniform
4. スーツを きる — wear a suit
5. あたらしい ふくを きる — wear new clothes

**Note:** Used for clothing worn on the upper body or full body (not hats, shoes, or accessories)

4. じかんに きを つける — be careful about time
5. ことばに きを つける — be careful with one's words

**Note:** Particle に indicates what you're being careful about
