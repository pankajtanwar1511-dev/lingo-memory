## Notes

1. **する-verbs** are formed by adding する to a noun. The noun can stand alone and be used independently.
2. **Example usage:**
   - Noun: 勉強が好きです (I like studying)
   - Verb: 日本語を勉強します (I study Japanese)
3. The two **core irregular verbs** (来る and する) don't have separate noun forms.
4. **Compound verbs** with 来る combine the て-form of another verb with 来る.
