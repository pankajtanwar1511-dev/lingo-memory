# Japanese Verbs and Phrases Reference

## Common Workplace Phrases

### Leaving the Office

**おさきにしつれいします** (osaki ni shitsurei shimasu)
- I'll be leaving now
- *Used when leaving the office before coworkers or boss*

**おつかれさまでした** (otsukaresama deshita)
- Thank you for your hard work
- *Said to someone who is leaving the office*

### Basic Expressions

**ちがいます** (chigaimasu)
- That's wrong / It isn't

**わかりました** (wakarimashita)
- Of course / No problem

**おねがいします** (onegai shimasu)
- Please

## Daily Life Verbs

### Work and Rest

**はたらきます / しごとします** (hatarakimasu / shigoto shimasu) - work

**やすみます** (yasumimasu) - rest, take a vacation, take a day off

**べんきょうします** (benkyou shimasu) - study

**かいものします** (kaimono shimasu) - do shopping

**せんたくします** (sentaku shimasu) - wash (clothes)

**そうじします** (souji shimasu) - clean, tidy up

### Sleep and Wake

**ねます** (nemasu) - go to bed, sleep

**おきます** (okimasu) - get up, wake up

### Start and End

**はじまります** (hajimarimasu) - start, begin

**おわります** (owarimasu) - finish, end

## Movement Verbs

### Basic Movement

**いきます** (ikimasu) - to go

**きます** (kimasu) - to come

**かえります** (kaerimasu) - to go home

**もどります** (modorimasu) - return

**あるきます** 歩きます (arukimasu) - walk [1G]

### Transportation

**のります** 乗ります (norimasu) - get on [1G]

**おります** 降ります (orimasu) - get off [2G]

**のりかえます** 乗り換えます (norikaemasu) - change (trains, etc.) [2G]

**（えきに）つきます** (駅に) 着きます (tsukimasu) - arrive [at a station] [1G]

**（ちゅうしゃじょうに）とめます** (駐車場に) 停めます (tomemasu) - park [in a car/bicycle park] [2G]

**うんてんします** 運転します (unten shimasu) - drive [3G]

**ドライブします** (doraibu shimasu) - drive

### Entry and Exit

**（へやに）はいります** (部屋に) 入ります (hairimasu) - enter [a room] [1G]

**（へやを）でます** (部屋を) 出ます (demasu) - go out [a room] [2G]

**（ふろに）はいります** (風呂に) 入ります (hairimasu) - take [a bath] [1G]
