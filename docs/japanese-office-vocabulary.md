## Legend

- 🔵 **Active** — vocabulary you will produce (speak or write yourself)
- ⚪ **Passive** — recognition only (reading / listening is sufficient)
- **Freq Tier**: S = Daily · A = Weekly · B = Monthly · C = Rare
- **Context tags** used in Notes: `[slack]` `[standup]` `[email]` `[meeting]` `[client]` `[hr]` `[engineering]` `[incident]` `[1on1]` `[keigo]` `[board]`
- `[exec-only]` — Low ROI for engineers / ICs; relevant mainly to management or legal roles. Skip in Phase 1.
- ⭐ Core business Japanese — high priority, widely used
- ★ Observed with high frequency in real workplace communications

> Active/Passive assumes a **mid-level engineer** at a tech company. The same word can be 🔵 for one role and ⚪ for another.
